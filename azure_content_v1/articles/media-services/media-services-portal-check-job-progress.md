## Use Portal to check the job status
1. In the [Azure Classic Portal](http://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/?LinkID=256666clcid=0x409), click **Media Services** and then click on the Media Services account name.
2. Select the **JOBS** page. 

    On the page you can see the status of  all your jobs. You can aslo filter by status, time, or job id.

    ![CheckStatus][checkstatus]
